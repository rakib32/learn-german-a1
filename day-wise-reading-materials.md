## 🎯 Exam Tips

- 🗣️ **Speaking** — speak slowly and clearly, it's okay to pause
- ✍️ **Writing** — check gender of nouns (der/die/das)
- 📖 **Reading** — read questions first, then the text
- 👂 **Listening** — focus on key words, not every word
- 📋 **Forms** — practice filling in personal info quickly
- 👋 **Greetings** — always start with a greeting in the speaking exam
- 💡 **If unsure** — use simple sentences, don't overcomplicate
